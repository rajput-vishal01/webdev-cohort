ABOUT ONE PIECE

One Piece revolves around Monkey D. Luffy, whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. With his crew of diverse and unique characters, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" to become the next Pirate King.

Monkey D. Luffy

The captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, known for his cheerful personality and incredible stretching abilities due to the Gum-Gum Fruit.

Roronoa Zoro

The swordsman of the crew, known for his three-sword fighting style and his goal to become the world's greatest swordsman.

Sanji

Sanji, the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates, is a master chef and skilled martial artist who uses a unique combat style based on powerful kicks called the Black Leg Style
